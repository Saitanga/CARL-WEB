The STEM strand in education refers to a specialized academic track that centers on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. This focused curriculum aims to provide students with an in-depth understanding of these disciplines, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Courses within the STEM strand often include advanced mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, computer science, and engineering-related subjects. By offering a concentrated and comprehensive approach to STEM education, the strand seeks to prepare students for future academic pursuits and careers in science, technology, engineering, or mathematics fields. This educational framework is designed to meet the growing demand for skilled professionals in STEM-related industries and encourages students to pursue paths that contribute to innovation and technological advancements.
