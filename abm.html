"ABM" stands for Accountancy, Business, and Management. It is an academic strand or track in some high school and senior high school systems, particularly in the Philippines. The ABM strand is designed to provide students with a foundation in business and management principles, preparing them for careers in fields such as accounting, finance, entrepreneurship, marketing, and business management. The curriculum typically includes subjects like accounting, business ethics, economics, finance, marketing, and organizational management. Students in the ABM strand develop skills in financial analysis, decision-making, and strategic planning, which are valuable in various business-related professions. The strand aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in the business world and related tertiary education programs.
